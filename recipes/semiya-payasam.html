Semiya Payasam

"Semiya Payasam" by Marvelous Kerala is licensed under CC BY-NC-ND 2.0 .

Description

Semiya Payasam is a traditional South Indian dessert made from vermicelli and milk. This is a quick recipe for 3 servings and can be made in just 5 minutes. It is best eaten hot.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice vermicelli
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 5 cashews, split
  • 5 raisins, deseeded
  • 1 pod cardamom, powdered
  • 2 tbsp ghee

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp ghee in a pan and roast the split cashews and deseeded raisins until golden brown.Remove them and keep aside.
  2. Add the rest of the ghee to the pan and add vermicelli. Fry until it starts to turn aromatic and light brown.
  3. Add room-temperature milk and bring to a boil.
  4. After it has fully boiled, remove pan from heat and add sugar and powdered cardamom.
  5. Mix well and wait a few minutes for the sugar to dissolve. Serve with fried cashews and raisins.
